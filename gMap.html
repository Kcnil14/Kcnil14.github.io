Treasure Hunting with Google Maps

The Hope Watermelon Festival holds a yearly treasure hunt with clues to the location of a hidden cash prize. Google Earth was used to identify potential prize locations based on participating sponsors and the content of the clues. The following map shows all participating sponsors, shown as light blue dots, as well as locations that could potentially be a match.
