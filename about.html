About Me

I obtained my B.A. and M.S. degrees in Geography from the University of Arkansas. I'm interested in a broad range of topics in GIScience and Remote Sensing. Topics include: web GIS, algorithm design and improvement, and analysis of remotely sensed imagery. Previous research focused on capturing provenance of workflows in the context of remote sensing and GIS.
